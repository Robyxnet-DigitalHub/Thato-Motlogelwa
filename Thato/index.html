Flow Cytometry

Flow cytometry is a sophisticated technique used in biology, immunology, and medicine to analyze and characterize cells based on their physical and chemical properties. By suspending cells in a fluid and passing them through a laser beam, flow cytometry measures the scattered and fluorescent light, providing valuable insights into cell structure, function, and behavior.

Mass Spectrometry

Mass spectrometry (MS) is a powerful analytical technique used to identify and quantify molecules in various samples, including chemicals, proteins, peptides, and metabolites. In mass spectrometry, molecules are ionized and then separated based on their mass-to-charge ratio.
